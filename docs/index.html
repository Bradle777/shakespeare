Computational Methods in the Humanities Final Project

Representation in Shakespeare: Language and Appearances

Introduction to the Project

Our project is reviewing several of Shakespeares works looking for trends in depictions of minoirty groups. Women for example, and finding attributes and descriptions of their role as wives, mothers, or in relation to a patriarchy. The depiction of black people is also of note throughout as we look for depictions of impurity assoicated with skin color.

Research Questions

  • What kind of descriptors are used to represent marginalized communties (women and racial minorities)?
  • Are there words that reappear in terms of describing women or racial identities? (seeming, more, pitch, etc.) How is purity determined?
  • What stereotypes are being perpetrated, implicitly or explicitly?
